# Instructions

1. **Extract the ZIP File**

   * Extract the contents of the ZIP file to the `D:\` drive.

2. **Open the Project**

   * Navigate to `D:\Hills`.
   * Open the `Hills.cbp` file using [Code::Blocks](http://www.codeblocks.org/).

3. **Build and Run**

   * In Code::Blocks, click **Build** to compile the project.
   * Click **Run** to start the program.

---

# How to Play

### Scene 1

* Click the **left mouse button (LMB)** to start the first scene.
* After the car exits the screen, press **j** to proceed to the next scene.

---

### Scene 2

* Click **LMB** to start the scene.
* When night falls and the bonfire starts, look at the boy’s pocket — you’ll see a **phone notification icon**.
* Press the following keys to interact with the messages:

  * **i** – View the first message.
  * **o** – View the second message.
  * **p** – View the final message.
* After reading all messages, press **j** to continue to the next scene.

---

### Scene 3

* Scene 3 is **automated** and will play without interaction.

---

## Quick Scene Navigation (Shortcuts)

* **l** – Skip to Scene 2 directly from Scene 1

* **q** – Jump to Scene 3 (Part 1)

* **a** – Jump to Scene 3 (Part 2)

* **z** – Jump to Scene 3 (Part 3)

---

## Additional Controls in Scene 2:

* **RMB** – Reset the scenario
* **k** – Stop the Jeep
* **c** – Enable night mode
* **v** – Enable day mode
* **s** – Start the sun
* **d** – Stop the sun
* **m** – Start the moon
* **n** – Stop the moon
* **w** – Start snow
* **e** – Stop snow
* **r** – Start phone ringing
* **t** – Stop phone ringing
* **f** – Start bonfire
* **g** – Stop bonfire

---

> ⚠️ All key presses must be in **lowercase** 🔤 .
